goal: provide a rich resource for Camp Lawton Scout Camp Staff for onboarding, training, and reference. 

Pillars (ordered by priority):

1. Interactive and robust Wiki providing Training (informational content like how-tos, links to official formal training), Policies and Procedures, Songbook, and Camp Culture And History. Should allow images and embedded code.   
   1. I have content from the staff handbook to seed the wiki with  
2. Deep WYSIWYG backend admin platform for Wiki editing, user management, application and onboarding processing, and user moderation.   
3. New staff application, onboarding, and links to training and required paperwork. An alerts HUD at the top with weather, fire, and news alerts and a ticker of scout appropriate entertainment feeds.   
   1. \*\*Required for ALL Staff (Volunteer & Paid):\*\*  
      1. \*   \*\*Staff Application\*\*.  
      2. \*   \*\*Letter of Agreement\*\*.  
      3. \*   \*\*Signed Code of Conduct\*\*.  
      4. \*   \*\*Annual Health and Medical Record (Parts A, B, and C)\*\*, which requires a physical exam completed by a licensed healthcare professional within the last 12 months.  
      5. \*   \*\*Vehicle Permit Form / Transportation Authorization\*\*.  
      6. \*   \*\*Venture Application / Leader Application\*\* (if you are not already registered).  
      7. \*   \*\*Background Check\*\* validation.  
      8. \*   \*\*Safeguarding Youth Training\*\*, which must be completed annually.  
      9. \*   \*\*Hazardous Weather Training\*\*, which must be completed online before any youth participants arrive.  
   2. \*\*Required for PAID Staff Only:\*\*  
      1. \*   \*\*I-9 Form\*\*, along with copies of two forms of I.D..  
      2. \*   \*\*IRS W-4 Form\*\*.  
      3. \*   \*\*Arizona Form A-4\*\* (State Withholding Election), which must be submitted within the first five days of starting work.  
4. Rich social forum for staff and alumni, coded from the ground up to be compliant with current scouting guidelines. 

UI/UX  
\* Modern and intuitive, capturing a rustic and utilitarian spirit. A Rustic LCARS.

* Palette: Dark brown, stone grey, forest green, yellow pine, charcoal, bold emergency warning red  
* Dark theme  
* Background subtle animation of campfire embers drifting upward.  
* Neurodivergent accessible  
  * Naturally high contrast  
  * Atkinson Hyperlegible and/or Inter fonts  
  * Dyslexia friendly mode (Lexie Readable and/or Opendyslexic)

Functionality

* Installable PWA web app   
* When ONLINE (active internet is detected), alerts, ticker, user management, forum, wiki editing are all actively functioning with up-to-the-minute updating (API polling limits notwithstanding)  
* When OFFLINE, the most recent cache of the *complete* wiki content and UI is stored locally, and the ticker shows locally stored content. Transition from online to offline is seamless. 

Critical rules

* Everything must be free and/or open source.   
* All functions and features must be in keeping with Scouting America guidelines.   
* Required official trainings and paperwork must be done through the official channels (fillable pdfs, secure file transfer to Catalina Council, DocuSign or open alternative, official trainings on my.scouting.org and Workplace Harassment Prevention for Employees: [https://www.skillsoftcompliance.com/Academy/Commonui/login.aspx?ReturnUrl=%2facademy%2fdefault.aspx%3forgid%3d551502\&orgid=551502](https://www.skillsoftcompliance.com/Academy/Commonui/login.aspx?ReturnUrl=%2facademy%2fdefault.aspx%3forgid%3d551502&orgid=551502))  
* Admin users are likely to NOT be tech savvy, so back end functionality must be intuitive and easy to use.   
* *I* am tech savvy but not a developer so I'll need detailed instructions for anything that can't be done through Google's Antigravity.
